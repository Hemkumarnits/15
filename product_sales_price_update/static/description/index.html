Update Bulk Product Price in Percentage

This app provide facility to update bulk product and product variant, sale prices on given percentage in the list view.

Screenshots

How to update sales price in Products (Product Template)

New Price = Base price + (Base price * Percentage)

How to update sales price in Product Variants

New Price = Base price + Variant Extra Price + (Variant Extra Price * Percentage)
